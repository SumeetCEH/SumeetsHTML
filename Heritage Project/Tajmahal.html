Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a magnificent white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Located in Agra, India, it is an epitome of love and is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Completed in 1653, it showcases Persian, Islamic, and Indian architectural styles.

Its stunning symmetry, intricate carvings, and beautiful gardens make it a masterpiece admired by visitors from around the world.
