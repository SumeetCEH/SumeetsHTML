Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, an epitome of love and beauty, is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century, this breathtaking white marble mausoleum was created in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, it stands as a symbol of India's rich Mughal heritage and artistic excellence.
